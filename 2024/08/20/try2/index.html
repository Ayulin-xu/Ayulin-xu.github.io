2024-08-20 Spanish

Definite Article And Noune

Nouns in Spanish are either masculine or feminine

  1. most Spanish nouns that end with -o are masculine, most end with -a are feminine.
  2. “the” is the only definite article in English. In Spanish, there are 4 ways to say “the”. Depend on whether the noun is masculine or feminine and whether is singlar or pluar.

The usage of definit article

  1. talk about something specific.
  2. talk about a noun in general.

3 rules make singular pural

  1. if a noun ends with a vowel, add -s.
  2. if a noun ends with a consonant(辅音) other than -z, add -es.
  3. if a noun ends with a -z, change the -z to -c and add -es.
